About Varna Priya

Where the cosmos meets the craft table

A Digital Sanctuary

Varna Priya is where the cosmos meets the craft table—a digital sanctuary for intricate mandalas, celestial art, and ethereal wallpapers. But it's more than just a portfolio. This is an exploration of the intersection between digital design and physical crafts, from glass-painted decor to stardust-infused crochet and jewelry.

Every piece created here is an invitation to bring celestial energy into everyday life, whether through the screen or in your hands.

Core Values

  • Authenticity: Every piece reflects genuine creative expression and cosmic inspiration
  • Accessibility: Beautiful art should be available to everyone, not locked behind paywalls
  • Craftsmanship: Quality over quantity, whether digital or handmade
  • Community: Building a space where cosmic souls can connect and find inspiration
  • Generosity: Giving value first, always

The Journey

Varna Priya began as a personal exploration of pattern, symmetry, and the beauty of the cosmos. What started as digital doodles evolved into intricate mandalas. From there, the journey expanded—applying those same cosmic principles to physical crafts, learning new techniques, and discovering the joy of creating with both pixels and paint.

This platform exists to share that journey, to offer resources that helped along the way, and to create a sustainable creative practice through the support of the community on Ko-fi.
